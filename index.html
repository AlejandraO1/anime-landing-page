Anime Recommendations

Bored? Pass the time by watching some animes!

  • Demon Slayer: Kimetsu No Yaiba


    A boy named Tanjiro Kamado, who had worked his whole life as a coal miner in order to help provide for his family, suddenly finds himself working for an organization called the Demon Slayer Corps. It specializes in killing demons. He does so in hopes of saving his little sister, Nezuko Kamado, from the life she now has to live.


    Watch it Here

  • Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion


    Lelouch vi Britiannia is high school student who also happens to be an exiled prince. After obtaining a power that makes people become absolutely obeidient of him, Lelouch takes it upon himself to use this power named Geass to lead a rebellion as Zero against the Holy Britannian Empire, the kingdom he was once an heir to.

    Watch it Here

  • Fruits Basket


    Tohru Honda is a high school girl who now finds herself homeless after the death of her mother. She ends up living with the most popular boy in school, Yuki Sohma as well as his cousins Kyo Sohma and Shigure Sohma. After accidently learning a big family secret of theirs, Tohru ends up becoming entangled in the complicated life of the Sohma family.

    Watch it Here

  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood


    Edward Elric is an alchemist who works for the military police. Despite his young age and short stature, he alongside his younger brother Alphonse Elric are on a mission to fix their grave mistakes. The brothers have a secret of how they obtained their alchemy and new looks: a secret that involves their huge mistakes and trying to play God.
